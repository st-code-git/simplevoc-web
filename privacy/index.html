Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

SimpleVoc is a vocabulary learning app that uses your voice to capture words and sentences, translates them using AI, and helps you memorise them through spaced repetition. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is protected.

1. Data We Collect

Data Why we collect it Stored
Email address Account identification and sign-in Firebase Auth
Display name (Google / Apple account) Shown in the app interface Firebase Auth
Vocabulary entries (words, sentences, translations) Core app content — your personal vocabulary collection On device Firestore
App settings (language preferences) Remembering your language configuration On device Firestore
Usage count (number of words added) Enforcing free-tier limits and premium entitlements Firestore
Voice audio Converted to text on-device by iOS speech recognition. The resulting text is sent to our AI service. Raw audio is never transmitted or stored. On device only
Spoken text Sent to Google Gemini API for word extraction and translation Google Gemini
Purchase & subscription status Verifying premium access RevenueCat
Anonymous device identifier Quota tracking before sign-in On device Firestore

We do not collect location data, contacts, browsing history, or any data unrelated to vocabulary learning.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Providing the service — saving and syncing your vocabulary across devices, delivering translations via AI, running spaced-repetition learning sessions.
  • Account management — authenticating you, managing your subscription status.
  • Improving the shared dictionary — anonymised word frequency data (not linked to your account) is aggregated to improve translation quality for all users.
  • Service integrity — Firebase App Check verifies that requests originate from legitimate app installations, protecting against abuse.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Location — cloud data is stored in Google Cloud's europe-west1 region (Frankfurt, Germany).
  • Encryption — all data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest by Firebase/Google Cloud.
  • Access control — Firestore security rules ensure each user can only access their own data. Backend services are protected by Firebase App Check and require authentication.
  • Retention — your data is stored as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time (see Section 6).

5. Children's Privacy

SimpleVoc is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — delete your account and all associated data directly in the app under Settings → Delete Account, or by contacting us. Deletion is permanent and takes effect immediately.
  • Portability — request your vocabulary data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. For significant changes we will notify users within the app.
